Get the Full Experience

No App Store needed. Install The Hitter's Ledger directly to your home screen in seconds.

iPhone Users

  1. Open The Hitter's Ledger in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share icon (the square with an up arrow).
  3. Select 'Add to Home Screen' from the menu.

Android Users

  1. Open the app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three dots in the top right corner.
  3. Tap 'Install App' or 'Add to Home Screen'.

Why no App Store? We built The Hitter's Ledger as a high-performance web app to ensure instant updates, zero tracking from big tech, and a smaller footprint on your device's storage.
